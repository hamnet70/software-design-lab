Lower School

Nurturing students in grades K - 3, our Lower School is dedicated to the whole child. We believe that even the youngest among us can be fine global citizens, so we begin by building awareness of others in the classroom.

Middle School

Spanning grades 4 - 7, the Middle School strives to harness all of the energy and drive our young students have into speaking out for themselves and others. We cultivate advocates, helping our students weigh their needs, understand their audience, and propose hoped-for outcomes.

Upper School

In grades 8 - 12, students learn more and more to be independent, in their activism, in their scholarship, and in their own development. Choice is key, as we offer independent studies and electives, encouraging each student to build her dream curriculum--one that challenges her heart and her mind.
