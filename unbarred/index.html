Welcome to Introduction to Digital Humanities. In this course you will get to explore how digital technologies can be use for humanities research and at the same time learn transferable, marketable technical skills. You will learn to build websites, apps, maps, games, and data visualization with HTML, CSS, Python, SQL, Jekyll and other tools. No prior knowledge is needed.
