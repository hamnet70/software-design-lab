To use an image in your digital humanities project, save it to your project folder. You can save the small image here, or click on an image to see it full size and save it from there.

Note that this page is built with "responsive design"—a layout that responds to the size of the screen or viewing area. To see it in action, resize this window by bringing your cursor to the lower, right-hand corner of the page. When your cursor turns into a two-headed arrow, click and drag the window to make it smaller, and watch the gallery respond.

Who took these photos? They all came from pixabay—a website where community members offer their photos for anyone to use, for nearly any purpose, without needing to credit or pay the artist. We in the education world believe that citing and crediting sources is one of the most important academic actions. We also appreciate sites like pixabay and their users who save us time, effort, and money.
